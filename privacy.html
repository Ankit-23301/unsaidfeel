Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 30, 2025

UnsaidFeel is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our emotional journaling web app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Address: Only used for account creation and login.
  • Emotional Entries: Stored anonymously unless user chooses otherwise.
  • Usage Data: We track usage to improve features.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To personalize your emotional feed and insights.
  • To keep your account secure.
  • To improve and optimize our services.

3. Data Security

We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore with security rules to protect your information. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. No Ads, No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with advertisers or other third parties.

5. Your Choices

You can delete your entries or account anytime. Contact us at unsaidfeel@gmail.com for assistance.

6. Children's Privacy

Users must be 13 years or older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted on this page.

By using UnsaidFeel, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Thank you for trusting us with your emotions.
